# Ecommerce-Customer-Retention-Analysis
End-to-end data analytics project using SQL, Power BI, Excel to analyze customer retention, sales performance, and business KPIs.

## Project Overview

| Item                 | Details                                                      |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Dataset**          | Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset                    |
| **Records Analyzed** | 100,000+ Orders, 96,000+ Customers                           |
| **Tools**            | SQL, Power BI, Excel                                         |
| **Focus**            | Customer Retention, Sales Performance, Business Intelligence |


This project analyzes an e-commerce marketplace dataset to understand customer behavior, sales performance, product trends, and operational efficiency.

Using SQL, Excel, and Power BI, the project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, KPI analysis, interactive dashboards, and business recommendations.

## Business Problem

E-commerce companies generate large amounts of transactional data, but without proper analysis it is difficult to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify revenue opportunities, and improve operational performance.

This project aims to answer key business questions related to customer retention, sales trends, product performance, and customer satisfaction to support data-driven decision making.

## Business Questions

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

* What is the overall sales performance of the marketplace?
* What percentage of customers become repeat buyers?
* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* How does customer purchasing behavior change over time?
* Which sellers contribute the most to sales?
* How do delivery times affect customer satisfaction and review scores?
* Which key performance indicators (KPIs) should management monitor to improve business performance?

## Dataset

The analysis is based on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset, which contains transactional data from a large online marketplace.

The dataset includes information about:

* Customers
* Orders
* Order Items
* Products
* Sellers
* Payments
* Customer Reviews
* Geolocation Data

The data was cleaned, transformed, and analyzed to support business reporting and identify actionable insights.

## Methodology

The project followed a structured data analytics workflow:

1. Explored and understood the database structure.
2. Cleaned and prepared the data for analysis.
3. Performed SQL queries to calculate key business metrics and answer business questions.
4. Analyzed customer behavior, sales performance, and operational trends.
5. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to communicate insights.
6. Developed business recommendations based on the analysis.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard focuses on the following business metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Repeat Customer Rate
* Conversion Rate
* Revenue by Product Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Customer Review Score
* Average Delivery Time

## Key Insights

The analysis identified several important business insights:

* Most customers make only a single purchase, indicating a significant opportunity to improve customer retention.
* Customer repeat purchase rates are relatively low compared to the total customer base.
* Product categories contribute differently to total revenue and average order value.
* Sales performance varies over time, highlighting seasonal trends and demand fluctuations.
* Delivery performance and customer review scores suggest opportunities to improve the overall customer experience.

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

* Develop customer retention strategies to increase repeat purchases and customer lifetime value.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories while improving underperforming ones.
* Monitor delivery performance to improve customer satisfaction and review scores.
* Track key business KPIs through interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision making.
* Continuously analyze customer purchasing patterns to identify new growth opportunities.
